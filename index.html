About us

We are a top class gym facility based in Leapordstown in South Dublin that have the machines, weights to suit your training. We have a group of personal trainers who carry out one to one and group sessions for people of all ages and fitness levels. We have a passion in achieving our clients fitness goals whether it be weight-loss or muscle gain.

We have a number of fantastic reviews from our clients which gives us huge satisfaction. We promise that we'll perform to our absolute maximum and to guide you on your journey whatever your goals and to help you MAKE YOUR MARK.
